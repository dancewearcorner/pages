    Your path into
    the DWC family.

    01

    Apply

    Choose Insider, Partner, or Referral and complete the short application — about 2 minutes. Tell us who you are and how you work with the dance community.

    02

    Get verified

    We confirm your status. Insider & Referral: 1–2 business days. Partner: 3–5 business days. Curated Referral pages go live within 5–7 business days.

    03

    Welcome in

    Confirmation email with your member dashboard, personal links, discount auto-applied at checkout, and access to community and education resources.

    04

    Save or earn

    Shop across all 13 brands at your member rate. Refer and earn 12% back. Partners: your Dress Code Store goes live within a week.

    What to have ready for verification

    Studio Insider

    A URL to a studio website where you're listed as faculty or educator, or a convention/competition listing showing you as faculty.

    Studio Partner

    Studio ownership document (proof of registered business entity), or a reseller license — whichever is most convenient.

    Studio Referral

    No documents needed. Sign up with email, tell us a little about you, and our team builds your curated page within 5–7 business days.

If you own a studio, Partner is almost always the right choice.
